About Me

I'm proficient in both C and C++ and I'm very comfortable working in C#, I know some HTML scripting as well. I'm an active person and as such I'm always doing some project or creating technical demos to learn and improve my programming skills.

Blender is my go-to 3D software tool but I can use other software such as 3D Max, Maya and Zbrush quite comfortably.I’m experienced with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

I love playing volleyball and I'm quite fond of astronomy and physics. I also love playing tabletop RPGs such as D&D, both as a player and as a game master.
